Welcome to Paradise

The Paradise Hotel is located on a tropical island somewhere in the Indian Ocean. The hotel has 150 rooms, two swimming pools, three restaurants, two bars and a night club.

Unspoiled beaches, clear blue sea and lush tropical gardens combine to make your stay a truly magical experience. Spend you day sailing, scuba diving, water skiing, exploring the island or taking one of our organized tours. The island has two world class golf clubs open to guests at the paradise Hotel.

In the evenings you can enjoy a romantic candle lite dinner at our roof garden restaurant then relax at the pool side bar.

The hotel is situated right on the beach front at the southern end of Paradise City. Shopping areas and the city center are 15 minutes by limousine from the hotel.
