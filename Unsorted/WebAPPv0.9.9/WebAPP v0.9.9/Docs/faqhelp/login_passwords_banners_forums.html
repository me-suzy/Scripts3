Q.  Where do I turn On or Off Banners?
A.  Follow these Links. Click "Site Admin". Click "Site Configuration" Click "Sections". Check or Uncheck the checkbox that reads "Banner Display". ""On version 9.5 click on "Admin Assistant" and look for the "Display Free Ad Block options" selector. This needs to be set to 1 or 2 for the banners to display.

   "" the "Display Banner" checkbox in "Sections" will be removed and the text "Display Free Ad Block options" will be changed to "Banner Display Options" in v0.9.6! ""
Q.  Where do I ADD, EDIT or DELETE a banner or banners?
Follow These Links. Click " Site Admin" Click "Banner Administration" this page will let you Edit or Delete Banners. To Add a Banner,  there is a Link below the table listing current banners called "Add Banner" Now That You are on "Add Banner" page you will see 5 textarea boxes. Just follow the example list provided.
  • Banner URL.......here you enter of where you want link to take you.
  • Image URL........this is where the banner is located. It could be on your server or some advertisers server it does not matter!
  • Image Width......self Explanatory
  • Image Height.....self Explanatory
  • Alt Content.......title of your link or a discription.

Q. Where do I Find My Forum Settings.?
A. Follow these links. Click " Site Admin" , Click "Site Configuration", Click "Forums (Posts). Here you will find the following controls.
  • Path to forums directory
  • Allow guestposting..checkbox
  • Max number of forumtopics..text
  • Max age of log files..text
  • Allow UBBC..checkbox
  • Path to messages directory
  • Allow notification..checkbox
  • Max number of forummessages..text
  • Insert original..checkbox
Q. Where I Set Up My New Forums ?
A. Follow these links. Click "Site Admin" scroll down and you will see a heading called "Forum Admin". The links will take you to setup your, Catagories, Boards and Censored words.
