Q. Where do I Find My "LINKS" Settings.?
A. Follow these links. Click " Site Admin" , Click "Site Configuration", Click "Links". Here you will find the following controls.
  • Path to links directory..text
  • Links only added by Admin..checkbox
  • Max number of links..text (i.e.30)
Link Catagory          back
Q. I want to add links to my links page. I've went to my links page and went to add links. I added a link but it only shows "There are x Links in our database" and my links are not showing up. What Do I Do ?
A. First Follow these Links. Click "Site Admin", Click "Link Administration". You will see a table called "Links Category" by default this is empty. You will need to setup catagories by clicking the link called "Add Link Category". Here you can setup your catagories for different types of links......i.e. Favorite Sites etc.

Q. Where do I Find My "Downloads" Settings.?
A. Follow these links. Click " Site Admin" , Click "Site Configuration", Click "Downloads". Here you will find the following controls.
  • Path to downloads directory..text

  • Max number of downloads..text (i.e.30)
  • Downloads only added by Admin..checkbox

Q. I want to add downloads to my downloads page. I went to my downloads page then went to "Add Downloads" page and added my download. When I went to check it out it reads "There is 1 downloads on our database" but there is no link to it. What must I do?
A. First Follow these Links. Click "Site Admin", Click "Download Administration". You will see a table called "Downloads Category" by default this is empty. You will need to setup catagories by clicking the link called "Add Downloads Category". Here you can setup your catagories for different types of downloads ......i.e. Graphics, Scripts etc.
