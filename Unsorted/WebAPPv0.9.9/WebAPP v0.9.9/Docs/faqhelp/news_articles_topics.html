Q. Where do I Find My "News (Articles)" Settings.?
A. Follow these Links. Click "Site Admin", Click "News (Articles)"
Here You Will find the following controls.
  • Path to topics directory..text
  • Max number of topics on topicscatpage..text
  • Allow HTML..checkbox
  • Max number of news on frontpage..text
  • Allow users to post articles..checkbox
  • Allow Guests to Comment..checkbox

Q. Where do I change my news(articles) topics Catagories?
A. Follow these links, Click "Site Admin" scroll down until you see the heading "News Admin" to change your catagories just click "Topic Categories". Once there you can add, edit or Delete catagories. Remember if your catagories don't fit in with the default images, you can upload an image file to your images/topics folder. Name the image to be consistant with your catagory.....
  All other items ( i.e.New Articles (publish/remove),News/Comments (edit/move/remove)) concerning Your News(articles) can be found under the heading "News Admin".
