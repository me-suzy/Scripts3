Q. Where I Find My Instant Message Controls ?
A. Follow these links. Click " Site Admin" , Click "Site Configuration", Click "Instant Messages". Here you will find the following controls.
  • Welcome Instant Message..checkbox
  • IM on New Article..checkbox
  • Broadcast Message Background Color..text (i.e.30)
  • IM on New Member..checkbox
  • Articles Editor..text
  • Broadcast Message Header Color..text

Q. Where do I find my "Statistics" controls ?
A. Follow these links. Click " Site Admin" , Click "Site Configuration", Click "Statistics". Here you will find the following controls.
  • Path to stats directory..text
  • Show top browsers..checkbox
  • Time between logging of IPs (minutes)..text
  • Show top OS...checkbox
