###########################
       INSTALLATION
###########################
upload
## 1 ##
the db folder and chmod the contents to 666
and the folder to 777.
## 2 ##
make a directory called logs and chmod it to 777
## 3 ##
upload the php files
chmod config.php and style.php to 777
## 4 ##
Go to http://yoursite.com/topsites/cpanel.php, login, the default username is admin password admin
## 5 ##
Upload the buttons you have with unique numbers, ie 1.gif 2.gif 3.gif.. as well as a default.gif
## 6 ##
Go to the general settings part of the contorl pannel and fill in the relivant details
## 7 ##
Go to http://yoursite.com/topsites/ and signup, you will require one account to allow your topsites to fully function.
## 8 ##
Modify the style settings within the control pannel!
