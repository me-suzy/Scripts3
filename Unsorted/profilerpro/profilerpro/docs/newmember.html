New Member
This feature allows you to create new users without using the New Applicant feature.
Alias The username or 'alias' of the member
Password The password of the member
Email The email address of the member
Rank The rank of the member. Your ranks are defined using the Settings feature.
Faction The faction or 'sub-division' that the member belongs to. Your factions are defined using the Settings feature.
Other Stats All other form fields are custom statistics you keep on your members, and are defined using the Settings feature.
