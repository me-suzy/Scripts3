How it Works

phpCheckoutTM is a digital marketing, selling and delivery application. It stores digital product, service or other resource listings which you enter into an online database. The products may be of any digital type including software, eBooks, music, movies, digital art, manuals, articles, certificates, newsletters, presentations, eMagazines, digital photo albums and any other resource that can be downloaded to a customer's computer, or a resource which is a digital service (Example: Search Engine Optimization). You set up the item and details about it in the database using the point and click Control Panel. You can toggle resources on or offline, change pricing, description or modify anything about the resource.

Visitors browse your resources using a showcase tool which can cycle through your listings, or alternatively link direct to the download method for the resource.
