Setting Up phpCheckout to work with PayPal IPN

To set up phpCheckout to work with PayPal Instant Payment Notification you need to configure phpCheckout and your PayPal account.

Steps for phpCheckout

  1. upload the separate pro*.php pages from your local machine to your web server /phpcheckout folder
  2. open the file called configure-dist-premium.php in a non invasive html editor (http://www.editplus.com is a good free editor)
  3. enter values for the following CONTANTS:
    • RECEIVER_EMAIL - an email address registered in your PayPal account - this is the paypal email account that funds will be credited to
    • PROCESSOR - the name of your processor - paypal or localhost
    • POSTTRANSACTIONSURL - the URL to post online payment transactions to using the built in form
    • USEREFERER - 'no' is for testing only, 'yes' will approve payments only FROM the REFERER you define below
    • REFERER - https://www.paypal.com/cgi-bin/webscr
    • NOTIFYADMINWHENSALE - if 'yes' is entered an email receipt for EVERY online sale is sent to the email address on the next line
    • NOTIFIYADMINADDRESS - The email address which will receive notification about sales if 'NOTIFYADMINWHENSALE' is 'yes', above
  4. save this file as 'configure.php' and upload to your web server

Steps for PayPal IPN

  1. log in to your secure PayPal account at https://www.paypal.com
  2. turn Instant Payment Notification on:

    Profile > Selling Preferences > Instant Payment Notification Preferences > Edit > check the checkbox and clear the url box

  3. click Save, and Log Out

Make a Test Payment

  1. Paypal advises to setup a Personal account, in addition to your Business or Premier account, in order to test the system
  2. _IF_ you have a second PayPal account then pay one account using the other account
  3. you may add a test item of $1 to your phpcheckout for this purpose

All done???

Congratulations, you're finished and can start earning $$$.
