About HookUp

What is HookUp?

HookUp is our feature which allows users to contact each other safely and anonymously. Here's how it works:

1. You see the picture of someone who has chosen to accept HookUps

2. You click the "HookUp" link on their picture page to send them a message. Your picture is attached to your message automatically (if you haven't uploaded a picture, you will need to do so).

3. If the recipient wants to HookUp with you, the two of you can communicate anonymously through our messaging system. Emails sent back and forth will show as coming from our site, and neither of your email addresses will be revealed to the other person.

To use HookUp, you must be a registered user of our site and upload a picture.
