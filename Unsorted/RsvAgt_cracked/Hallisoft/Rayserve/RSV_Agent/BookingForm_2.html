City Hotel - Booking Form

Enter your requirements into the form below. Press the "Calculate" button to show the total cost for your stay. Press "Book this Room" to submit this booking, "Add to List" to add the booking to your booking list, or "Confirm All" to submit your booking list to our reservation server.

  • The total cost of your stay will include any meals and extras that you specify on the booking form.
  • All prices include value Added Tax (VAT).
  • Meal prices are charged per person per day.

  • Reservations cannot be accepted unless supported by a valid credit card. You will be transferred to our secure server before being asked to enter your card number.
