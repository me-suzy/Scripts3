The demo application attempts to show the versatility of Rayserve and the features available. Hotel 1 uses the seasonal matrix, rates are charged per room and the maximum amount of information is displayed in the booking form. Hotel 2 uses the daily matrix, rates are charged per person and minimal information is show in the booking form. Note: All bookings will appear in the same booking list.

The Paradise Hotel

Uses the seasonal matrix and charges are per room per night with no child discount.

The City Hotel

Uses the daily matrix and charges are per person per night with child discounts based on children sharing with a set number of adults.
