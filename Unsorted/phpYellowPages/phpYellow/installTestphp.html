Trouble Shooting?

  • if php is not working you may ask your internet service provider to enable it
  • php is free to download from http://www.php.net
  • if you can not see your specific php configuration information then open util.php and check your INSTALLPATH value.
  • if you still can not see your specific php configuration information then open util.php and check your ADMINUSER and ADMINPASSWORD values are both temporarily set to "admin".
