  General report  

This report will provide you with information about general amount of your visitors, leads and clients as well as your general expenditure and income for each of your campaign and action.

Report could be generated for all campaigns to get only general information and to compare all mentioned above activities for different campaigns, or for one specified campaign to get detailed report about this campaign.

To specify if the report should be generated for all campaigns or only for one choose the proper parameter in campaigns selection list.
All – for all campaigns.
Campaign name – for specific campaign.

You could also set the time period for which the report will be generated. The default time period is “Last week”, choose the proper time from time period select list or specify the period you need, manually, by using time setting controls.
