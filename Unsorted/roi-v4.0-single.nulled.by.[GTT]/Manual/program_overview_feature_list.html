  Feature list  
  • Setup within a few minutes
    Step-by-step installation makes the ROI TRACKING PRO really easy to set up. No programming knowledge is needed to configure the script.
  • Intuitive and adjustable interface
    Easy-to-use, intuitive Web interface allows you to administer the product from any location with web access. You could also set the interface resolution according to your monitor settings.
  • Keywords tracking
    The script can track keyword based campaigns (ex. Google Adwords) on any search engine providing you with a full statistic on how your keywords listings and separate keywords doing.
  • Track any marketing campaign
    The script can track advertising on any click source (PPC ad on Google, Overture etc.) as well as banner campaigns or sponsored links in web archives or catalogs.
  • Multiple web site monitoring
    One script can handle unlimited amount of publicized sites.
  • Campaign wizard
    Campaign wizard will lead you through new campaign creating process step-by-step assisting you with complete and detailed instructions on each step.
  • Automatic code generation
    No programming knowledge needed to adjust the script for tracking your marketing campaigns. All necessary code will be generated automatically, you will copy and paste it on your pages.
  • Multifunctional campaigns and actions management
    Convenient and easy-to-use Web interface of admin area supported with step-by-step wizards will help you to modify any existing campaign or action in no time.
  • Database data storing.
    The script works with MySQL database and supplied with customizable database size control tool to protect it from overflow.
  • Calculate Return on Investment (ROI)
    The ROI Tracking pro will calculate how much revenue you generate per dollar spent on advertising.
  • Unique and general clicks statistic.
    You could get the statistic of unique visitors and general traffic for each campaign or action tracked by the ROI Tracking Pro.
  • Client statistic
    The ROI Tracking Pro will generate statistic of all clients - paying visitors, by each sale action and advertising campaign and calculate your visitor to client conversion ratio.
  • Lead statistic
    Get the statistic of leads - visitors that filled your information request form, watched your product demo or performed any other sales leading actions on your site for any of your campaign and action. The system also determine your visitor to lead conversion ratio.
  • Campaign comparative analysis
    With campaign comparative reports you will easily find out which of your advertising investments are driving your business and which consider revising.
  • Complete financial report
    You will be provided with detailed information about your expenditures and incomes per each visitor, each action and each campaign.
  • No traffic limitations
    The ROI Tracking Pro provides affordable ROI statistics to small, medium and big size businesses. One script can handle unlimited amount of campaigns/actions.
  • Export to CSV format
    The script supports export of your reports to CSV format using the determination symbol on your choice, to make your reports easier to transfer to other statistic programs.
  • Print function
    You could make a print copy for any of generated reports.
  • Cookie based tracking system
    Script tracking system is based on cookies with configurable expiration time.
  • Advanced filtering and sorting options including filtering by date range.
    With ROI Tracking Pro you could get daily, weekly, monthly or annually statistic of your visitors. You could generate the report for any manually-defined time period as well. Filtering by campaign name and sorting data by any parameter on your choice is also available.
  • Flexible script configuration
    You could configure the script to meet your personal business needs by configuring the rules for report creating and tuning the Web interface.
  • Password protected access to admin area.
    The script is protected by password to avoid any unauthorized data or configurations changes, or data drain.
  • Priority support 24/7
    The ROI tracking Pro is a licensed product and is being supported with 24/7 technical assistance center. We guarantee that you will get full and detailed instructions on solving any problem with product installation, configuration and utilization.
