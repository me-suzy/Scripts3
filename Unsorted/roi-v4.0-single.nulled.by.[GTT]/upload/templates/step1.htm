This wizard will help you to create a campaign to track effectiveness of your advertising  ROI (Return On Investment).

What the campaign is?

In the ROI tracking Pro you could create a campaign for each of your advertising that refers to your site. Every campaign has a referrer link  the link that is used to track and count visitors that comes to your site from this referrer. For each campaign you could create several actions such as form filling, questionnaire filling, order making and etc. This will help you to track different actions of your ad visitors.
