Navigating:
  • Click on either a category or an article from the left-hand pane and it will be displayed in the main window.
  • Use the Dropdown in the upper right to easily view all articles in a specific category.
  • Use the Menu at the top of the main page and subsequent pages to easily move between major Knowledge Base areas.
  • Search, which is not available at this time, allows users to easily identify relevant articles from the many articles available.
